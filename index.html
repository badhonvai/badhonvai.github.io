WHO VAI IS

Badhon vai is a leading leader of Bangladesh Chhatra League, JESSORE which is one of the two major political parties of Bangladesh. He is young and has uncompromising integrity.

On January 4, 1948, Bangladesh Chhatra League was established by the Sheikh Mujibur Rahman at the meeting of Fazlul Huq Hall of Dhaka University, this organization was established through a meeting organized by the student leaders. It was named East Pakistan Muslim Chattra League during its establishment. Naeemuddin Ahmed was the first convener of BCL.

During the 1971 Liberation War, the members of the Chhatra League were recruited into Mujib Bahini, an armed group trained by RAW, aimed at fighting against the leftist freedom fighters and to bar them from taking the lead in the war. Mujib Bahini was accused of being involved in hooliganism and looting after the end of war by AK Khandokar.
